KNOW MORE ABOUT GODLIKE



Majorly team Godlike earns from the tournament prize pools, and the total earnings till date are approximately $183,464.Godlike Esports is an Indian Esports Organization. It was formed in 2018 just after the first official pubg tournament was announced. It has an interesting story behind its name. There was a clan in mini militia with the name Godlike and when the game of pubg was launched, the members started playing under the same name Godl or Godlike. Team GODLIKE BGMI, owner, origin, roster, achievements, history, net worth.

The owner of Godlike Esports is Kronten aka Chetan Chandgude. He is a famous streamer and BGMI player. He was also a part of the first roster which entered a tournament as he himself was a PUBG player.The current lineup of Godlike is considered one of the best in India and is recognised as a competition by the leading teams in global tournaments. It is the most experienced indian when it comes to global tournaments. Most of the teams try to avoid taking fights with the team as the team is fearless and confident and have the skills and synergy to back their aggression.A lot of players have been part of team Godlike. Among them the most successful and impactful lineup are “Smxkie”, “Aladin”, “Rayed”, “Haster” and “Cartoonz”, “ScoutOP”, “Daljitsk”.
